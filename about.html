Our Journey

From a small advisory firm to a trusted investment partner

2007 - Foundation

BR Jalan Securities was established with a vision to provide personalized investment advisory services to individual investors in Mumbai.

2010 - SEBI Registration

Obtained SEBI registration (INZ000199136) and expanded our services to include portfolio management and institutional advisory.

2015 - Technology Integration

Launched our digital platform and mobile app, providing clients with real-time portfolio tracking and market insights.

2020 - Pan-India Presence

Expanded operations across major Indian cities, serving over 2,500 clients with assets under management exceeding ₹450 crores.

2025 - Innovation Hub

Launched our 10th Collective Investment Scheme and established an innovation lab for developing AI-driven investment strategies.
